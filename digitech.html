My Programming and Web Design Projects.

Below is a list of the digi projects I have undertaken, as well as a short blurb on their purpose.

FLORIS

FLORIS was my year 9 Programming assessment.

Tasked to create a python turtle program to create a geometric flower-making program. Going down a rabbit hole, I added optional row after optional row, sepals, borders and the like.

An excercise in development, at least.

StreakIt

A habit tracking PySimpleGUI app.

In 2025 I wanted to create an app with a GUI that could be downloaded and stored locally.

I created a simple app with a data.txt file that data is written to as the app is closed, and a simple layout so people can record their habits everyday and build routines.

Wairarapa Foraging

In 2024 I made this website as my web design assessment.

It took a while to find an idea, but once I thought of this, I worked on creating a small encyclopedia of forageable goods.

Very enjoyable, though a little basic. One of my first projects.

Mushrooms

Grows like a vegetable and tastes halfway like meat, growing symbiotic with trees or eating away at their rotting corpses, mushrooms are an essential part of the web of life.
Mushrooms are one of the most nutritious and delicious things to forage. From meaty giant puffballs found in fields, some big enough to feed a family to small delicate shiitake mushrooms growing on poplar and oak, mushrooms are a great forage for an experienced forager.

REMEMBER!
Some mushrooms can be very dangerous, and only pick mushrooms after you have learned the dangerous ones and when you are ABSOLUTELY sure what mushrooms you are picking.

About This

This page is all about what this project is, why I made it, and if I will be continuing it.
My name is Finn-Oskar Mikkelsen, I am 15 years old, and I am interested in Maths, Digital Technologies, Permaculture, Theatre, and Music. After school, I hope to pursue a double degree in Mathematics and either Physics or Computer Science.

What?

This was a school project I made for my Digital Technologies 1.2 Assessment. After brainstorming, a friend recommended a foraging info website. I took this onboard, and have been trying to complete it for the assessment.
I filled it with information about some of my favourite things to forage in the Wairarapa. However, I've never been the best with deadlines, and as I am working overtime you can expect to see some filler test below.
I do hope to be able to finish this website someday, however.

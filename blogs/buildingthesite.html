Building the Site

Who am I?

Hi, I'm Finn-Oskar, a year 11 student with a passion for DigiTech, Drama, Maths and Agriculture.
I'm making this website as an opportunity to:

  • Make some money through freelancing and tutoring.
  • Develop my writing and journalling skills through managing this blog.
  • Showcase and develop my design, programming and performance skills in a project-based manner.

I'll be writing about my garden using permaculture principles, the projects I'm working on, reviewing (and promoting!) my drama performances, and recounting the interesting things that happen to me.

How did I Build This Website?

This site is hosted on GitHub pages, and built from scratch using HTML5 and CSS3.
It's taken me a while to build it, and it will probably be weeks until Google indexes it and it becomes publicly available (I'll probably have to promote it a bit to get it into your hands).
